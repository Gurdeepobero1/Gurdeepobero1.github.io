  • 6:45-7:00 AM: Refresh + Breakfast
  • 7:00-8:00 AM: High-Priority Study (60 min)

  • 5:40-6:30 PM: Revision + Notes (50 min)

  • 8:00-9:00 PM: Secondary Topics (SSB prep, current affairs)

Emergency Protocols

If Behind Schedule

  • Focus only on high-priority topics (General Engineering, OR).
  • Reduce fitness to 30 min/day.
  • Increase study to 7 hours if necessary.

If Health Issues

  • Reduce study to 4 hours.
  • Prioritize rest and light exercise.
  • Focus on revision, not new topics.

Study Motivation Crisis

  • Watch GATE Topper interviews.
  • Connect with study groups.
  • Take a 1-2 day complete break.
